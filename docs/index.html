Solid · 2.0 · Strategy04 / 12
Product vision · Solid 2.0

A premium learning library that answers "what should I learn today?" — in three minutes.

01 · Target
Mid-career professionals inside modern companies — B2B primary. Curious self-directed learners — B2C secondary.
02 · Value
Three minutes of signal a day. A library when you want more. A coach when you're stuck.
03 · Position
Between an LMS (too heavy) and TikTok (too light). Editorial, not encyclopedic.

Visual hierarchy rules
→One hero, always. Never two.
→Pills = round. Reels = vertical. Series = rectangle. Podcasts = circle.
→Editor picks over algorithm, 1 row in 5.
→No infinite scroll. Six rows. That's the day.
→Coach lives on the right. Never in the middle of content.
Solid · 2.0 · Strategy07 / 12
AI coach

A companion, not a feature.

Recommend
Based on role, path, calendar and what you just finished. "What's the one pill for today?"
Summarize
TL;DR of any video. Turn a series into a one-page playbook.
Answer
Ask questions about a specific chapter. Cites the pill by timestamp.
Coach
Nudges before meetings. Prompts after. Never shames.
Suggest next
Builds your next path, in your cadence, from the library.
Coach · live
"You have a 1:1 with Pablo on Thursday. Based on last week's notes, 'Feedback: act, don't label' is 3 minutes well spent. Add to today?"
